About PDF BOX

Useful PDF tools without sending your documents away

PDF BOX is a free collection of browser-based tools for everyday PDF work: combining documents, extracting pages, changing orientation, converting formats, and more.

How it works

When you choose a file, the tool processes it locally in the browser using JavaScript libraries. This means the document remains on your device instead of being uploaded to a PDF BOX server. This approach is especially useful for files that contain personal, academic, or business information.

Who it is for

PDF BOX is designed for students preparing assignments, professionals organizing reports, small businesses handling routine documents, and anyone who needs a straightforward PDF task completed without an account.

What we value

  • Clear tools that describe what they do before a file is chosen.
  • Local processing whenever the browser supports it.
  • Free access without a required sign-up.
  • Useful help so people can check their output with confidence.
