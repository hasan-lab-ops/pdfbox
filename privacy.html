Privacy Policy

The short version: your files never leave your device. Here's the longer version.

Last updated: July 2026

Your files

Every tool on this site — merge, split, compress, organize, convert, watermark, page numbers, PDF to Word, Word to PDF — runs entirely as JavaScript inside your own browser. When you "upload" a file to a tool, it's read directly off your device into your browser's memory; it is never sent to a server, because this site doesn't have one to send it to. Closing or refreshing the tab discards everything.

What we don't collect

  • No accounts, no sign-up, no login
  • No analytics or tracking scripts
  • No cookies set by this site
  • No copies of your files, ever, anywhere

Third-party resources this page loads

To render and process files, this page loads a handful of open-source code libraries (pdf-lib, pdf.js, JSZip, and docx) and web fonts from third-party content delivery networks (cdnjs, unpkg, and Google Fonts) when you first open it. Fetching those files means your browser contacts those providers directly, the same way it would to load an image from any other website — those requests carry your IP address and basic request metadata, standard for any resource loaded from a CDN. Your documents are not part of that request and are never transmitted to them or to us.

Contact form / email

The contact page opens your email client (or Gmail's compose window) addressed to us. Anything you choose to send in that email is between you and your email provider — this site has no part in it.

Changes to this policy

If this page's practices ever change (for example, if a future version added analytics), this policy will be updated to reflect that. Since this is a static site with no backend, there's no mechanism to silently change what happens to your files without also changing the code you can read yourself.

Questions

If anything here is unclear, reach out on the contact page.
