About Us

Welcome to PDF Toolkit, your secure, free, and fast platform for managing PDF files.

Our mission is to provide simple yet powerful tools to merge, split, compress, and edit PDFs without compromising your privacy. Unlike many other services that require you to upload your sensitive documents to their servers, our tools leverage modern web technologies to process your files directly in your web browser.

Our Commitment to Privacy

Your privacy is our top priority. Because all processing happens locally on your device, we never see, store, or share your files. Your data remains strictly yours.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

At PDFBox, the privacy and security of your data are our top priorities. We build our PDF tools so users can process documents safely and confidently without exposing sensitive files to unnecessary risk.

1. Information We Collect

We collect as little information as possible. Our priority is to keep your data private and minimize any personal information stored or processed.

  • Files and Documents: We do NOT store or access any PDF files, images, or documents you process with our tools. All uploaded items are processed securely and are removed immediately after the conversion or merging workflow completes.
  • Processing Security: When files are handled through any communication channels, we use fully encrypted and secure protocols. This ensures that data remains protected during the processing session.
  • Log Files: We may use standard server logs for monitoring site health. These logs can include IP address, browser type, ISP, date/time stamp, and referring pages. This information is not linked to personally identifiable information.

2. Cookies and Web Beacons

Like many websites, PDFBox uses cookies to improve user experience and remember settings. Cookies may store information about visitor preferences and the pages visited. We do not use cookies to track personal identity.

3. Security and Data Retention

Your files are treated with strict confidentiality. Any uploaded document or generated result is automatically and permanently deleted as soon as the processing session is complete. We do not retain document contents, and no third party can access your files after the task finishes.

4. Advertising Partners

Third-party ad networks may use cookies, JavaScript, or web beacons to serve ads and analyze performance. These third parties may receive your IP address when ads are displayed. PDFBox does not control these technologies and is not responsible for their privacy practices.

5. CCPA & GDPR Privacy Rights

Under CCPA and GDPR, residents may request disclosure of the categories of personal data collected. Because we do not collect or retain personal files through our document tools, these requests are generally not applicable to processing workflows. If you have questions about cookies or analytic data, please contact us at hasan.saad898@gmail.com. We will respond within one month.
