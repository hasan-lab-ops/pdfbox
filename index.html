About Us

Welcome to PDF Toolkit, your secure, free, and fast platform for managing PDF files.

Our mission is to provide simple yet powerful tools to merge, split, compress, and edit PDFs without compromising your privacy. Unlike many other services that require you to upload your sensitive documents to their servers, our tools leverage modern web technologies to process your files directly in your web browser.

Our Commitment to Privacy

Your privacy is our top priority. Because all processing happens locally on your device, we never see, store, or share your files. Your data remains strictly yours.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

At PDF Toolkit, accessible from our website, one of our main priorities is the privacy of our visitors. This Privacy Policy document contains types of information that is collected and recorded by PDF Toolkit and how we use it.

1. Information We Collect

Because our core services are designed around client-side processing, we deliberately collect as little information as possible:

  • Files and Documents: We do NOT collect, upload, store, or transmit any PDF files or documents you process using our tools. All processing is executed locally within your web browser's memory.
  • Log Files: Like many standard web servers, we use log files. These files merely log visitors to the site. The information collected includes internet protocol (IP) addresses, browser type, Internet Service Provider (ISP), date and time stamp, referring/exit pages, and possibly the number of clicks. These are not linked to any information that is personally identifiable.

2. Cookies and Web Beacons

Like any other website, PDF Toolkit uses 'cookies'. These cookies are used to store information including visitors' preferences, and the pages on the website that the visitor accessed or visited. The information is used to optimize the users' experience by customizing our web page content based on visitors' browser type and/or other information.

3. Google DoubleClick DART Cookie

Google is one of a third-party vendor on our site. It also uses cookies, known as DART cookies, to serve ads to our site visitors based upon their visit to our site and other sites on the internet. However, visitors may choose to decline the use of DART cookies by visiting the Google ad and content network Privacy Policy at the following URL – https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads.

4. Advertising Partners Privacy Policies

Third-party ad servers or ad networks uses technologies like cookies, JavaScript, or Web Beacons that are used in their respective advertisements and links that appear on PDF Toolkit, which are sent directly to users' browser. They automatically receive your IP address when this occurs. These technologies are used to measure the effectiveness of their advertising campaigns and/or to personalize the advertising content that you see on websites that you visit. Note that PDF Toolkit has no access to or control over these cookies that are used by third-party advertisers.

5. CCPA & GDPR Privacy Rights

Under the CCPA and GDPR, among other rights, consumers have the right to request that a business that collects a consumer's personal data disclose the categories and specific pieces of personal data that a business has collected about consumers. Since we do not collect personal data or retain user files, these requests are generally not applicable to our document processing, but you may contact us regarding any cookies or analytic data. If you make a request, we have one month to respond to you. If you would like to exercise any of these rights, please contact us at hasan.saad898@gmail.com.
