Before you email

Remember that no file you process on Fathom is ever sent anywhere — so if something looks wrong with a converted file, a screenshot or a description of what you tried is far more useful to us than the file itself (and there's no way to attach it through the buttons above regardless, since this site was built with exactly that kind of privacy in mind).
